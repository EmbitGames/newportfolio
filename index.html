Solo Projects

Unicellular

Unicellular is an idle entity simulation game where the player purchases different Unicells that eat, fight and die to produce currency. This project is capable of handling thousands of entities with finite state machines and interactions.

Technologies: Unity 2D, Finite State Machines, Optimisation Techniques

Pinnable

My first official Unity project, a 2D platformer where players collect falling marbles that are bouncing from moving pins. Inspired by Peggle, the player needs to avoid molten hazards and survive to achieve a highscore.

Technologies: Unity 2D, Difficulty scaling systems
